Karmic Patterns & Cycles — Common Questions

Karmic patterns are recurring themes — in relationships, career, health, or life events — that trace back to specific planetary placements in your birth chart. They show what you're predisposed to experience repeatedly until the underlying cycle shifts.
Some patterns soften through awareness and conscious effort. Others have defined durations tied to planetary periods. A consultation clarifies which patterns you can influence and which ones you need to navigate with preparation.
Different life events often share the same underlying planetary trigger. Your chart reveals the common root beneath seemingly different problems — which is why addressing surface symptoms alone doesn't resolve the pattern.
Karmic cycles are governed by dashas (planetary periods) that range from 6 to 20 years for major periods, with sub-periods within them. Timing analysis maps exactly where you are in the cycle and when shifts are expected.
