Founder, Life of Lotus

Hi, I'm Saumya.

I built Life of Lotus from lived experience, not philosophy, not theory.

Astrology entered my life through loss, confusion, grief, and the long work of rebuilding. Clarity collapsed. Decisions felt paralysing. I questioned my sense of self and direction. Strength didn't arrive as a breakthrough, I rebuilt it deliberately through disciplined inner work, choices made without certainty, and the slow act of trusting myself again.

Life of Lotus emerged from that ground.

Astrology gave me a framework when logic and modern approaches to healing left me lost. When emotions overwhelmed, it imposed structure. When life fragmented, it restored perspective. More importantly, it revealed why certain events unfold despite resistance, and how to move through them with awareness, dignity, and responsibility, without surrendering agency.

That understanding defines how I practice today.

I draw my work from an uncommon intersection: years in product management, training in social sciences and business, over a decade of Vipassana practice, and sustained inquiry into how planetary movements mirror human experience.

I offer grounded, no-nonsense guidance. We translate insight from your birth chart into perspective, timing, and deliberate action. Each session creates mental order, emotional steadiness, and decisiveness. You leave equipped, with practical frameworks that strengthen clarity, courage, and conscious choice.

Astrology is a precise, deeply structured system. It maps time, cycles, consequence. Watching it diluted into shortcuts or irresponsible practice is painful, especially as that erosion leads people to question its very relevance.

I care deeply about restoring its integrity.

I built this practice to return astrology to its rightful place, as disciplined, responsible work that respects both human agency and intelligence. I built this work with intention. I continue it with responsibility. I offer it with respect, for the individual seeking guidance, and for the knowledge itself.
