Common Questions

What are planetary remedies in Vedic astrology?

Planetary remedies are practices, mantras, donations, and lifestyle changes that strengthen positive planetary influences and reduce negative effects in your birth chart. They include daily rituals, day-specific practices, mantras, and behavioral changes aligned with each planet's nature.

Which planet should I focus on first?

Start with the planet causing the most visible challenges in your life. Common starting points: Moon for emotional issues, Saturn for delays and discipline problems, Rahu for confusion and obsession, and Sun for confidence and recognition issues. A birth chart analysis can pinpoint your priority.

How long do planetary remedies take to work?

Most remedies show initial effects within 40-90 days of consistent practice. However, deeper shifts in planetary patterns can take months or even years. The key is consistent, sincere practice rather than expecting quick fixes.

Can I do remedies for multiple planets at once?

Yes, but start with 1-2 planets and add gradually. Quality matters more than quantity. It's better to do thorough remedies for one planet than scattered efforts for many. Day-specific practices help organize multiple remedies.

Do I need to know my birth chart for planetary remedies?

Not necessarily for general strengthening. However, for targeted remedies addressing specific life challenges, knowing your birth chart helps identify which planets need attention. A consultation can reveal your unique planetary priorities.
