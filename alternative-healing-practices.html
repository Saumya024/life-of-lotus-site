Alternative Healing & Consciousness Practices

Healing takes many forms. Some work through the body, others through breath, sound, or stillness. Some are rooted in ancient traditions, others in modern research. What they share is a respect for the body's capacity to restore itself when given the right conditions.

This section offers clear, practical overviews of practices I've encountered and found valuable, either through personal experience or through practitioners I trust. Use it to orient yourself, ask better questions, and find what resonates with where you are now.
