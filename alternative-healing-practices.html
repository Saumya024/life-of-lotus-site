Alternative Healing & Consciousness Practices

This section comes from my long-standing respect for alternative healing practices and for the practitioners who uphold them thoughtfully. It is offered as a space for awareness and appreciation, of these traditions, and of those who continue them with seriousness and care. If it helps even a few people orient themselves more clearly in their search, it has served its purpose.

These practices differ in method, depth, and tradition. This section offers clear, neutral overviews to help you understand how each works and where it may be useful.
