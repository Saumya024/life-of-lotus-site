Privacy Policy

I Read Space respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you visit the I Read Space website or engage with its services.

By using this website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information that is necessary to provide our services responsibly and thoughtfully.

Information you voluntarily provide

When you fill out an intake form, contact us, or request a session, you may share personal details such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact number
  • Country or location
  • Birth details
  • Any information you choose to share as part of your query or context

This information is shared voluntarily and is used solely for consultation-related purposes.

Automatically collected information

When you visit the website, limited technical information may be collected automatically, such as:

  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • General usage statistics

This information helps maintain the website and understand how it is used. It does not personally identify you.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only for purposes directly related to the services offered, including:

  • Reviewing your intake details before a session
  • Communicating with you regarding bookings, scheduling, or clarifications
  • Providing thoughtful and relevant guidance during sessions
  • Maintaining basic records for continuity and follow-up, if needed

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

Confidentiality & Data Care

All personal information shared is treated with care and discretion.

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to protect your information from unauthorised access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Sharing of Information

Your personal information is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Information may be disclosed only if required by law, regulation, or a valid legal request, or if necessary to protect rights, safety, or comply with legal obligations.

External Links

This website may contain links to external websites. I Read Space is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external websites you visit. Some links may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information you have shared
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information, where appropriate
  • Ask questions about how your information is handled

Requests can be made by contacting us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in services or legal requirements. The most current version will always be available on the website.
