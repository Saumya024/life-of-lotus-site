Refund Policy

I Read Space operates on a time- and preparation-based consultation model. For this reason, all session bookings are non-refundable.

Once a session is booked, time is reserved and preparatory work may begin. As such, we are unable to offer refunds under any circumstances, including changes in personal availability or unforeseen situations.

We encourage you to review the session details carefully and ensure your availability before confirming a booking. If you have any questions or need clarification about the nature of the sessions, you are welcome to reach out before booking.

For concerns related to scheduling, please refer to the Rescheduling Policy.

By completing a booking, you acknowledge and agree to this refund policy.
