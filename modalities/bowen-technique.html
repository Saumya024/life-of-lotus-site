Bowen Technique

Manual, Physical & Circulatory Therapies

How It Works

  • A practitioner makes gentle rolling moves over muscles, tendons, and fascia
  • Moves are made in specific locations, then the practitioner pauses to allow the body to respond
  • Sessions include waiting periods where the practitioner leaves the room briefly
  • The technique works with the body's own healing intelligence

What People Typically Experience

  • Deep relaxation during sessions
  • Relief from pain and tension, often after just one or two sessions
  • Improved mobility and function
  • Resolution of issues that hadn't responded to other treatments
  • A sense of the body self-correcting

Who This Is For

  • People who prefer gentle approaches
  • Those whose conditions haven't responded to stronger techniques
  • Anyone wanting to work with the body's self-healing capacity
  • People sensitive to touch who find other bodywork too intense

The pauses allow the body to integrate the moves without interference. The body does its own processing during these breaks.
