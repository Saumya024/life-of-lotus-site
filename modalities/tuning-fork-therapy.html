Tuning Fork Therapy

Sound, Mantra & Vibrational Practices

How It Works

  • Tuning forks of specific frequencies are struck and placed on or near the body
  • Different frequencies are used for different purposes
  • Vibration is transmitted directly into tissues or the energy field
  • Forks may be used in sequence or specific combinations

What People Typically Experience

  • Precise vibration in targeted areas
  • Relief from pain or tension
  • Sense of energy shifting or realigning
  • Deep relaxation from specific frequencies
  • Clarity and calm from particular tone combinations

Who This Is For

  • People who want precise, targeted sound therapy
  • Those interested in the science of frequency healing
  • Anyone seeking a simple, portable sound healing tool
  • People who find other sound tools too intense

Frequently Asked Questions

What frequencies are used?
Various frequencies for different purposes. Common ones include 528 Hz (believed to support healing) and forks based on planetary frequencies.
Can tuning forks be used on acupuncture points?
Yes, this is common. Sound acupuncture uses forks instead of needles on the same points.
Is this evidence-based?
Some research supports vibrational therapy. Specific frequency claims have less research backing.
Can I learn to use tuning forks on myself?
Yes, with basic instruction. Many people use them for self-care.
How is this different from other sound healing?
Tuning forks offer precise, single frequencies applied directly. Other methods involve more complex sound environments.
What should I look for in a practitioner?
Training in tuning fork protocols, understanding of frequencies, and experience with conditions like yours.
