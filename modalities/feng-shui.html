Feng Shui

Astrology, Cosmology & Time–Space Systems

How It Works

  • A practitioner assesses how energy flows through your space using compass directions or the bagua map
  • Each area of your home corresponds to a life area like wealth, relationships, or career
  • Recommendations address clutter, furniture placement, colors, elements, and symbolic objects
  • The goal is optimizing energy flow to support your intentions and wellbeing

What People Typically Experience

  • Feeling calmer and more organized in their space
  • Improved energy levels and motivation
  • Better sleep quality after bedroom adjustments
  • Shifts in stagnant life areas after focused changes
  • Greater sense of harmony and control over their environment

Who This Is For

  • People who feel drained or stuck at home
  • Those moving to a new space who want to start with good energy
  • Anyone seeking to enhance specific life areas through environmental change
  • People sensitive to their surroundings who want tools to optimize them

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to believe in chi for Feng Shui to work?
Whether you call it chi, energy, or simply good design psychology, the practical effects of an organized, balanced space are real. You don't need specific beliefs to benefit.
What is the bagua map?
It's a grid dividing any space into nine areas, each corresponding to a life aspect: wealth, fame, love, family, health, creativity, knowledge, career, and helpful people.
Is there a best Feng Shui school?
Different schools (Form, Compass, Black Hat, etc.) have varying approaches. What matters most is finding a practitioner whose method resonates with you and gets results.
Can Feng Shui really improve my finances?
It creates an environment that supports focus and intention. Combined with action, this can help—but it's not magic. The environmental shift supports your efforts rather than replacing them.
What's the biggest Feng Shui mistake people make?
Clutter is the most common issue. Before any other adjustments, clearing out unused items typically has the biggest impact on how a space feels and functions.
How often should I reassess my space?
Any major life change warrants reassessment. Otherwise, seasonal adjustments and regular decluttering maintain good energy flow.
