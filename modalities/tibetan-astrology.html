Frequently Asked Questions

How is Tibetan astrology different from Vedic astrology?
While both have Indian roots, Tibetan astrology incorporates Chinese elements and has evolved within Buddhist culture. It often includes spiritual considerations like karma and rebirth.
Do I need to be Buddhist to use Tibetan astrology?
Not at all. While it developed in Buddhist culture, the practical insights about timing, compatibility, and life patterns are useful regardless of religious background.
What kind of events do people use Tibetan astrology for?
Traditionally it's consulted for marriages, travel, moving homes, starting projects, and medical treatments. Any significant life event can be timed using this system.
Is Tibetan astrology connected to Tibetan medicine?
Yes, they share the same elemental framework. Tibetan doctors often consider astrological factors when diagnosing and treating patients.
How do I find a qualified Tibetan astrologer?
Look for practitioners trained in traditional lineages or at Tibetan institutions. Many are found within Tibetan Buddhist communities or at Tibetan medical centers.
What's the significance of elements in Tibetan astrology?
The five elements (wood, fire, earth, metal, water) cycle through years and affect everything from personality to health. Balance between elements is considered essential for wellbeing.
