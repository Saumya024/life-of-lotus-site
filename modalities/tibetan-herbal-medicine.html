Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Chinese medicine?
Though related historically, Tibetan medicine developed its own diagnostic methods, formulas, and integration with Buddhist practice.
Are Tibetan herbs available outside Tibet?
Some are cultivated elsewhere; many authentic formulas come from Tibetan medical institutions in India and Nepal.
Is quality reliable?
Purchase from established Tibetan medical organizations for best quality. Traditional preparations follow precise methods.
Can I use Tibetan medicine for serious conditions?
Traditionally used for many conditions. For serious illness, integrate with modern medical care.
What are the three humors?
Wind, bile, and phlegm—similar to but distinct from Ayurvedic doshas. Imbalance in these causes disease.
Do I need to be Buddhist?
No religious requirement. The medicine is practical healing, though Buddhist context may deepen understanding.
