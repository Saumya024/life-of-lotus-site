Frequently Asked Questions

How is Pranic Healing different from Reiki?
Both work with life energy. Pranic Healing has specific scanning and cleansing techniques; Reiki is more intuitive and uses attunement transmission.
Why is there no touch?
Pranic Healing works in the energy field surrounding the body. Physical touch isn't needed to affect this field.
Can I learn Pranic Healing for self-healing?
Yes, courses teach self-healing techniques. Many people start with self-practice.
Is this scientifically proven?
Some research supports energy healing generally. Pranic Healing specifically has limited mainstream research but many testimonials.
What conditions can Pranic Healing help?
Practitioners use it for physical ailments, emotional issues, and stress. It's complementary to medical care.
How long is a session?
Typically 30-60 minutes depending on what's being addressed.
