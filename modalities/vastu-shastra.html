Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Vastu and Feng Shui?
Both are space arrangement sciences, but Vastu originated in India and emphasizes direction and planetary influence, while Feng Shui is Chinese and focuses more on energy flow. They have overlapping principles but different frameworks.
Do I need to make major structural changes?
Not necessarily. Many adjustments involve simple changes—moving furniture, adding elements, using colors, or placing specific objects. Major structural changes are suggested only when simpler remedies aren't sufficient.
Can Vastu help with relationship problems?
It addresses the southwest area of your home, associated with relationships. Corrections there can create more supportive energy, though relationship work still requires direct attention.
What if my home's main door faces the wrong direction?
Various remedies exist for directional challenges. Complete perfection isn't required—the goal is improving balance within existing constraints.
Is Vastu connected to any religion?
It developed in ancient India and has connections to Hindu temples and architecture. However, its principles about space and energy can be applied regardless of religious background.
How quickly do Vastu changes take effect?
Some people notice shifts within days, others over weeks or months. It depends on the nature of the imbalance and how thoroughly recommendations are implemented.
