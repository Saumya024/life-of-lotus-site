  • Practitioners assess the state of your seven major energy centers
  • Various techniques—sound, visualization, hands-on work—balance each center

  • Sessions may focus on particular centers or address the whole system

  • Deeper connection to intuition and inner knowing

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the seven chakras?
Root (security), sacral (creativity/emotions), solar plexus (power), heart (love), throat (expression), third eye (intuition), crown (spirituality).
How do I know if my chakras are blocked?
Practitioners may assess through sensing, pendulums, or discussion. Patterns in your life often reflect which centers need attention.
Can I balance my own chakras?
Yes, through meditation, visualization, sound, and movement. Many resources teach self-care practices.
Is this scientific?
The energy centers correspond somewhat to nerve plexuses and endocrine glands. The model is primarily experiential rather than scientifically validated.
How often should I have chakra work done?
As needed, often when you feel stuck in a particular life area or during significant life transitions.
Are chakras religious?
They come from yogic and tantric traditions. Working with them practically doesn't require religious belief.
