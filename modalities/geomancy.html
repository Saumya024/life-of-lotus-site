Geomancy

Astrology, Cosmology & Time–Space Systems

How It Works

  • Patterns are generated through various methods—traditionally, marks made in sand or on paper
  • Sixteen basic figures are interpreted based on their position and combination
  • Questions can be asked about specific situations, similar to other divination systems
  • The practice reads the patterns as reflections of hidden influences and likely outcomes

What People Typically Experience

  • Direct answers to specific questions and situations
  • Insight into hidden factors affecting their circumstances
  • Validation of intuitions they couldn't quite articulate
  • Practical guidance for decisions and timing
  • Access to a tradition that feels grounded and earth-connected

Who This Is For

  • People seeking direct guidance on specific questions
  • Those drawn to earth-based spiritual practices
  • Anyone interested in divination systems beyond tarot or astrology
  • People who appreciate practical, straightforward answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How is geomancy different from tarot or astrology?
Geomancy uses simple binary figures rather than cards or planetary positions. It's particularly good for yes/no questions and specific situations rather than broad personality analysis.
What kind of questions work best?
Specific, focused questions get the clearest answers. Rather than 'What should I do with my life?', ask about a particular decision, timing, or situation.
Is this related to dowsing or ley lines?
Modern use of the word 'geomancy' sometimes refers to earth energy work, but traditional geomancy is a divination system. They share the 'geo' (earth) root but are distinct practices.
How old is this practice?
It originated in the Middle East and spread to Europe and Africa during medieval times. It has a long history across multiple cultures.
Can anyone learn geomancy?
Yes, the basic method is straightforward to learn. Interpretation takes practice, but the fundamentals are accessible to anyone interested.
How accurate is geomancy for predictions?
Like all divination, skill matters. Experienced practitioners often report high accuracy for specific questions, especially regarding timing and hidden influences.
