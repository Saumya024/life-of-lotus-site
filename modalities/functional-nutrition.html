Functional Nutrition

Plant, Herbal & Nutritional Therapies

How It Works

  • Assessment looks for root causes rather than just treating symptoms
  • Testing may examine gut health, hormones, genetics, and nutrient status
  • Protocols address underlying imbalances causing symptoms
  • Treatment integrates diet, supplements, and lifestyle modifications

What People Typically Experience

  • Understanding of connections between symptoms they didn't realize were related
  • Resolution of issues through addressing root causes
  • Personalized protocols rather than generic recommendations
  • Improved gut health affecting whole-body wellness
  • Greater energy and vitality as systems come into balance

Who This Is For

  • People with complex health issues
  • Those frustrated with symptom-focused treatment
  • Anyone wanting to understand why they feel the way they do
  • People interested in the connections between body systems

Frequently Asked Questions

What's functional about functional nutrition?
It focuses on how systems function and interact, addressing root causes rather than isolated symptoms.
How is this different from regular nutrition advice?
It looks deeper at biochemistry, gut health, hormones, and individual variation. More testing and personalization is typical.
What kind of testing is involved?
May include gut microbiome analysis, food sensitivity panels, hormone testing, nutrient status, and genetic factors.
Is this evidence-based?
It draws on research, though some protocols are ahead of mainstream adoption. The field is growing in scientific support.
How long are treatment protocols?
Often months, addressing layers of imbalance systematically.
Can this help with autoimmune conditions?
Many functional practitioners focus on autoimmune conditions, addressing gut health, inflammation, and triggers.
