Therapeutic Touch

Energy & Subtle-Body Practices

How It Works

  • A practitioner moves hands through your energy field, usually without physical contact
  • The field is assessed for areas of imbalance
  • Energy is modulated through intentional hand movements
  • Sessions are typically brief, often 20-30 minutes

What People Typically Experience

  • Relaxation and reduced anxiety
  • Pain reduction
  • Accelerated wound healing in some cases
  • Sense of balance and calm
  • Relief from stress-related symptoms

Who This Is For

  • People in healthcare settings
  • Those seeking quick, non-invasive energy work
  • Anyone wanting a well-researched energy therapy
  • People who prefer approaches used by nurses and healthcare workers

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Therapeutic Touch have research support?
It's one of the most researched energy therapies, with studies on anxiety, pain, and wound healing.
Why doesn't the practitioner touch me?
The work happens in the energy field around the body. Contact isn't needed to affect this field.
Is this the same as Healing Touch?
Related but distinct. Therapeutic Touch came first; Healing Touch developed from it with additional techniques.
Who typically practices this?
Many practitioners are nurses. It was specifically developed for healthcare settings.
How quickly does it work?
Many people feel effects during or immediately after the brief session.
Can this help with anxiety?
One of its most-researched applications is anxiety reduction, especially in medical settings.
