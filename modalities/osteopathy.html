Frequently Asked Questions

How is osteopathy different from chiropractic?
Both work with the musculoskeletal system. Osteopathy traditionally uses gentler techniques and emphasizes the body as a whole system. Chiropractic often focuses on spinal adjustments.
Is osteopathy safe?
Yes, when practiced by qualified practitioners. Techniques are generally gentle. Inform your osteopath of any conditions or concerns.
How many sessions will I need?
Depends on your condition. Acute issues may resolve in 2-4 sessions. Chronic problems typically need more.
Can osteopathy help with non-musculoskeletal issues?
Some practitioners address digestive, respiratory, and other issues through their effects on body structure. Results vary.
What training do osteopaths have?
In many countries, osteopathy is a regulated profession requiring years of training. In the US, DOs are fully licensed physicians.
Is osteopathy covered by insurance?
Coverage varies by location and plan. Check with your insurer.
