  • Therapies include herbal preparations, dietary changes, and physical treatments

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the four humors?
Blood, phlegm, yellow bile, and black bile—corresponding to different temperaments and health tendencies. Treatment aims to balance these.
Where is Unani practiced?
Primarily in South Asia, the Middle East, and Muslim communities worldwide. India has formal Unani medical education and hospitals.
How is Unani different from Ayurveda?
Though both exist in South Asia, Unani comes from Greek-Islamic tradition with different theoretical foundations. They share some herbs and approaches.
Are Unani medicines safe?
When prepared and prescribed properly, yes. Quality control is important—seek qualified practitioners and reputable sources.
Can Unani treat chronic diseases?
It's traditionally used for various chronic conditions. Work with qualified practitioners and maintain conventional medical care for serious conditions.
What training do Unani doctors have?
In countries like India, formal degrees (BUMS) require years of study. Look for licensed practitioners.
