Frequently Asked Questions

Are herbs safe?
When used appropriately, many are very safe. Some are powerful and require knowledge. Quality and sourcing matter. Consult a trained herbalist.
Can herbs interact with medications?
Yes, some can. Always inform your doctor about herbal use, especially with serious conditions or medications.
How long until herbs work?
Some act quickly; others need weeks or months. Herbs typically work gradually, supporting the body rather than overriding it.
Can I just buy herbs at the health food store?
For simple, common uses, yes. For chronic conditions, professional guidance helps you choose the right herbs and dosages.
What's the difference between herbalism traditions?
Western herbalism, Ayurveda, Chinese medicine, and others have different frameworks and herb selections, though there's overlap.
Are herbs evidence-based?
Many have significant research support. Others have traditional use without modern studies. The field is actively developing scientific evidence.
