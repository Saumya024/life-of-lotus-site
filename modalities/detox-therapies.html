Frequently Asked Questions

Does detox actually work?
The body constantly detoxifies through liver, kidneys, and other pathways. Supportive practices may enhance these processes. Some specific claims are exaggerated.
What are detox symptoms?
Headaches, fatigue, skin breakouts, and irritability can occur as the body adjusts. These typically pass within days.
Are detox products necessary?
Many natural methods work well—fasting, whole foods, saunas. Commercial products vary in quality and necessity.
How often should I detox?
Seasonal cleanses (1-4 times yearly) are traditional. Daily supportive habits may reduce need for intensive detoxes.
Is colon cleansing part of detox?
Some protocols include colonics or enemas. These are not necessary for everyone and carry some risks. Use with qualified guidance if interested.
Who should avoid detox protocols?
Pregnant women, those with eating disorders, people on medications, and individuals with certain health conditions should get medical guidance.
