Frequently Asked Questions

What are nadis?
In yogic tradition, nadis are subtle energy channels through which life force flows. The three main ones are ida, pingala, and sushumna.
How is this different from Reiki or Pranic Healing?
Nadi healing specifically works with the yogic nadi system. Other modalities have their own energy frameworks.
Do I need yoga experience?
Not required, though familiarity with yogic concepts may help you understand the approach.
How many sessions are needed?
Depends on the issues being addressed. Some people feel shifts immediately; patterns may need more sessions.
Can I learn to work with my own nadis?
Yes, practices like pranayama and certain meditations help keep nadis clear.
Is this connected to any religion?
It comes from yogic tradition but is practical energy work, not religious practice.
