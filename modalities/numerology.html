Frequently Asked Questions

What's a Life Path number and how do I calculate it?
Add all digits of your birth date until you reach a single digit (or master number 11, 22, 33). For example, March 15, 1990 = 3+1+5+1+9+9+0 = 28 = 2+8 = 10 = 1. Your Life Path is 1.
Does changing my name change my numerology?
Yes, the name you actively use affects your Expression energy. Many people who change names notice shifts in how they present themselves and how others perceive them.
Are master numbers special?
Numbers 11, 22, and 33 are considered master numbers with intensified energy. They carry more potential but also more challenges than single digits.
How accurate is numerology compared to astrology?
They offer different perspectives. Numerology provides quick, accessible insights while astrology offers more detail. Many practitioners use both together.
Why does my name matter in numerology?
Your name carries a vibration based on its letters (each assigned a number). The name given at birth is significant, but names you use also matter.
Can numerology predict specific events?
It's better at identifying themes and timing windows than specific predictions. Personal year cycles show what kind of energy is available, not exactly what will happen.
