Frequently Asked Questions

What practices fall under this category?
EFT, TFT, TAT, and various other techniques working with meridians for emotional healing.
Can I learn these myself?
Many techniques are designed for self-use. More complex applications benefit from practitioner guidance.
Are these approaches mainstream?
They're growing in acceptance. Research is building, and they're used by many therapists and coaches.
How do meridians relate to emotions?
In Chinese medicine, each meridian relates to specific emotional patterns. Working with meridians affects associated emotions.
What conditions respond to these approaches?
Commonly used for anxiety, phobias, trauma, stress, and self-limiting beliefs.
How long does change take?
Often surprisingly quick for specific issues. Patterns may shift in minutes, though complex issues take longer.
