Frequently Asked Questions

Is Shaktipat safe?
With a qualified, ethical teacher, yes. The main risk is with pseudo-teachers or when not ready for the experience.
Do I need to follow a specific path?
Shaktipat comes from Indian traditions but the energy transcends specific practices. Most teachers do work within particular lineages.
Can Shaktipat happen remotely?
Some traditions include distant transmission. Effectiveness may vary.
What if nothing happens?
Not everyone has dramatic experiences. Subtle shifts may unfold over time.
How do I find a legitimate teacher?
Look for established teachers with clear lineages. Be cautious of anyone making grandiose claims or demanding inappropriate commitment.
Is this the same as Kundalini awakening?
Shaktipat can trigger or accelerate Kundalini awakening. They're related but not identical.
