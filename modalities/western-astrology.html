Western Astrology

Astrology, Cosmology & Time–Space Systems

How It Works

  • Your birth chart is calculated using the tropical zodiac, which aligns with the seasons rather than star positions
  • The chart reveals your Sun sign, Moon sign, rising sign, and the positions of all planets at birth
  • A practitioner interprets how these placements interact and what they suggest about your personality and life themes
  • Current planetary transits are tracked to understand present-day influences

What People Typically Experience

  • Deeper self-understanding beyond just their Sun sign
  • Insight into emotional patterns through Moon sign analysis
  • Clarity on how they come across to others through their rising sign
  • Recognition of recurring relationship dynamics
  • Better timing awareness for major decisions

Who This Is For

  • People curious about astrology who want to go deeper than magazine horoscopes
  • Those seeking psychological insight through a symbolic language
  • Anyone navigating major life transitions and wanting perspective
  • People interested in understanding relationship compatibility

Frequently Asked Questions

What's my Sun sign vs Moon sign vs rising sign?
Your Sun sign reflects your core identity, Moon sign shows your emotional nature and inner world, and rising sign (or ascendant) indicates how you present yourself to others. Together they paint a much fuller picture than Sun sign alone.
Why doesn't my Sun sign description feel accurate?
Your birth chart contains many other influences that may overshadow or modify your Sun sign expression. Your Moon sign, rising sign, and other planetary placements all contribute to who you are.
How accurate is Western astrology?
Accuracy depends heavily on the skill of the practitioner and the specificity of your questions. Good astrology provides insight and timing guidance rather than fortune-telling.
Can astrology tell me who I should marry?
Astrology can reveal compatibility patterns and potential challenges between two charts. It offers insight rather than absolute answers—human relationships are complex and involve choice.
Do I need my exact birth time?
For the most accurate reading, yes. Without it, your rising sign and house placements can't be determined. Some readings can still be done, but they'll be less precise.
What are planetary transits?
Transits are the current positions of planets as they move through the sky. When they aspect (form geometric angles to) points in your birth chart, they activate those areas of your life.
