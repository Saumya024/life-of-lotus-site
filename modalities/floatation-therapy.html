Floatation Therapy

Technology-Assisted & Complementary Practices

How It Works

  • You float effortlessly in a tank containing water saturated with Epsom salt
  • Water temperature matches skin temperature, reducing body awareness
  • The tank is dark and quiet, minimizing all sensory input
  • Sessions typically last 60-90 minutes

What People Typically Experience

  • Profound relaxation and stress release
  • Elimination of external stimulation
  • Muscle relaxation from floating weightlessly
  • Meditative or altered states of consciousness
  • Deep rest that persists after sessions

Who This Is For

  • People with chronic stress or anxiety
  • Those with pain or tension seeking relief
  • Anyone wanting meditation without effort
  • People curious about sensory deprivation experiences

Frequently Asked Questions

Is floating claustrophobic?
Tanks are larger than people expect. You control the lid and lights. Most people feel expansive rather than confined.
What if I fall asleep?
Many people do. The salt keeps you floating safely. Drowning isn't a realistic risk.

How often should I float?
Weekly to monthly is common for ongoing benefits. Some people float more during stressful periods.
What should I do during the float?
Nothing is required. Let go, relax, let your mind do what it wants. There's no wrong way to float.
