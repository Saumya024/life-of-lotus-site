Frequently Asked Questions

What are the Akashic Records?
They're described as an energetic library containing all information about every soul. It's a metaphysical concept, not scientifically verified.
Can anyone access the Records?
Training and practice help, but many believe everyone has access. Some people are naturally more attuned.
What can I ask about?
Common topics include life purpose, relationships, career, health patterns, and understanding life challenges.
Is this the same as psychic reading?
Related but distinct. Akashic readings specifically access this Records framework. Psychic reading can use various sources.
How do I find a reliable reader?
Look for trained, experienced readers with clear ethics and good testimonials. Trust your intuition about the connection.
How accurate are Akashic readings?
Accuracy varies by reader and question. They're often valued more for insight and perspective than specific predictions.
