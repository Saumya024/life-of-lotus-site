Tibetan Singing Bowls

Sound, Mantra & Vibrational Practices

How It Works

  • Metal bowls are struck or rubbed to produce resonant tones
  • Bowls may be placed on or around the body
  • The vibrations affect both physical tissues and energy field
  • Sessions typically include multiple bowls of different tones

What People Typically Experience

  • Deep relaxation and nervous system calming
  • Feeling of vibration passing through the body
  • Altered states of consciousness
  • Release of tension and stuck energy
  • Profound peace and stillness

Who This Is For

  • People who respond well to sound healing
  • Those seeking deep relaxation without effort
  • Anyone curious about vibrational medicine
  • People who find visual or mental meditation difficult

Frequently Asked Questions

Do the bowls need to touch my body?
They can be placed on or near the body. When placed on the body, vibration is felt more directly.
Are all singing bowls the same?
No. Quality, composition, and tone vary significantly. Antique bowls are particularly valued but modern bowls can be effective.
Can singing bowls help with pain?
Many people report pain relief. The relaxation and vibration may help with various conditions.
Is this scientifically supported?
Research is limited but growing. Effects on brainwaves and stress markers have been documented.
Can I use singing bowls at home?
Yes, with basic instruction. Playing for yourself offers ongoing benefits.
