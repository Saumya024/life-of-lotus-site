How It Works

  • Cards, coins, or other objects are used to generate guidance

  • The process invites reflection beyond ordinary thinking

Frequently Asked Questions

How are oracle cards different from tarot?
Tarot has fixed structure (78 cards, specific archetypes). Oracle cards are created freely with any theme, imagery, or number of cards.
Can oracle cards predict the future?
They're better for insight and reflection than specific predictions. They help you understand yourself and your situation.
How do I choose a deck?
Choose one whose imagery speaks to you. The personal connection matters more than any system.
Can I create my own oracle deck?
Absolutely. Many people create personal decks for their own use.
How do the cards know?
The mechanism is debated—synchronicity, projection, intuition. What matters is whether the guidance is useful.
How often should I use oracle cards?
Many people pull daily cards for reflection. Use as often as feels helpful.
