Energy & Subtle-Body Practices

How It Works

  • A practitioner uses specific techniques to balance your energy field
  • Hands may be placed on or near the body
  • The approach was developed for use in nursing and healthcare settings
  • Sessions are structured around particular energy patterns and goals

What People Typically Experience

  • Deep relaxation and stress reduction
  • Pain relief, especially in hospital settings
  • Faster recovery from surgery or procedures
  • Reduced anxiety before medical treatments
  • A sense of being cared for and supported

Who This Is For

  • People in healthcare settings wanting complementary support
  • Those undergoing medical treatments
  • Anyone seeking gentle, professional energy work

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Healing Touch used in hospitals?
Yes, it's one of the most widely used energy therapies in healthcare settings. Many nurses are certified.
How is this different from Therapeutic Touch?
They're related. Therapeutic Touch was developed first; Healing Touch expanded on it with more techniques.
Is it appropriate before surgery?
Often used for pre-surgical anxiety and post-surgical recovery. Inform your medical team about any complementary treatments.
How many sessions help?
Even one session can reduce anxiety and pain. Series may be helpful for ongoing support during treatment.
Can anyone learn this?
Yes, certification courses are available. Many healthcare professionals and laypeople train in Healing Touch.
Is this covered by insurance?
Rarely covered directly, but sometimes offered as part of integrative hospital programs.
