Sound Baths

Sound, Mantra & Vibrational Practices

How It Works

  • Various instruments—bowls, gongs, chimes, tuning forks—create an immersive sonic environment
  • You lie down and receive the sounds passively
  • No effort or concentration is required
  • Sessions may include guided relaxation or simply instrumental sound

What People Typically Experience

  • Deep relaxation without trying
  • Quieting of mental chatter
  • Physical sensations of vibration
  • Altered states—time distortion, visualization, insights
  • Rejuvenation and calm that persists after the session

Who This Is For

  • People who find active meditation difficult
  • Those seeking stress relief without effort
  • Anyone curious about sound healing
  • People wanting a group experience without interaction

Frequently Asked Questions

What instruments are used?
Commonly: singing bowls, gongs, chimes, tuning forks, drums. Sometimes voice. The combination varies by practitioner.
What should I wear?
Comfortable clothing—you'll be lying down for 30-60 minutes typically.
Can I fall asleep?
Yes, it's common and fine. Benefits occur regardless.
Is this like meditation?
It's more passive than most meditation. You receive rather than focus. The sounds do the work.
How do I find quality sessions?
Look for experienced practitioners with good reviews. The quality of instruments and the skill of playing matters.
Can I do this at home?
Recordings exist, though live sessions have different impact. You can also learn to play instruments yourself.
