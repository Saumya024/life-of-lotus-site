Emotional Freedom Technique

Energy Psychology & Tapping

How It Works

  • You tap with your fingers on specific acupuncture points while focusing on an emotional issue
  • A setup phrase acknowledges the problem while affirming self-acceptance
  • Several points on the face, upper body, and hands are tapped in sequence
  • The process often reduces emotional intensity within minutes

What People Typically Experience

  • Rapid reduction in emotional distress
  • Relief from anxiety, fear, and stress
  • Changes in long-held emotional patterns
  • Physical relaxation as emotional charge releases
  • Ability to address difficult issues more calmly

Who This Is For

  • People wanting quick techniques for emotional relief
  • Those dealing with anxiety, phobias, or stress
  • Anyone interested in self-help energy tools
  • People who want approaches they can use independently

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly does EFT work?
Often within minutes for specific issues. Complex patterns may need repeated sessions.
Can I do EFT on myself?
Yes, it's designed for self-application. Basic technique is easy to learn.
Is EFT evidence-based?
Growing research supports it for anxiety, PTSD, and pain. It's one of the more researched energy psychology techniques.
What are the tapping points?
Key points include eyebrow, side of eye, under eye, under nose, chin, collarbone, and under arm. Some protocols include others.
Can EFT replace therapy?
For simple issues, it may be sufficient. For complex trauma or conditions, professional support alongside EFT is recommended.
Why combine tapping with speaking about the issue?
The combination seems to rewire the emotional charge connected to the issue. Speaking activates the pattern; tapping disrupts it.
