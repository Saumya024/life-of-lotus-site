Frequently Asked Questions

How can such diluted substances work?
This is homeopathy's controversial aspect. Proponents say it works through energy or information; critics say it's placebo. Many patients report genuine improvements.
Is there scientific evidence?
Research is mixed. Some studies show effects; others don't. It remains contentious in mainstream medicine despite widespread use.
Are homeopathic remedies safe?
The remedies themselves are extremely dilute and considered safe. The risk is in delaying effective treatment for serious conditions.
How long until I see results?
Some people respond quickly; others need time. Constitutional treatment of chronic issues typically unfolds over months.
Can I use homeopathy with conventional medicine?
Generally yes, as the remedies don't interact pharmacologically. Always inform all your healthcare providers.
What should I expect in a consultation?
An extensive interview—1-2 hours initially—covering all aspects of your symptoms, personality, preferences, and history.
