A structured behavioral reset built from psychological astrology.

It doesn't diagnose you or predict events. It helps you identify one repeating friction pattern and design a small, disciplined interruption around it — sustained for 43 days.

No grand promises. Just a clear structure and a commitment to follow it.

Short bursts create motivation. Sustained repetition creates change.

Forty-three days is long enough to outlast enthusiasm, resistance, and distraction — but short enough to commit to without drama. Most people quit between Day 12 and Day 21. This structure pushes beyond that drop-off point.

It's behavioral, not symbolic.

You identify one domain. You define one daily action (15 minutes or less). You set a fixed time and minimum standard. You follow it for 43 days. You recalibrate on Day 7.

The action is small on purpose. Consistency builds structural force. Momentum comes from repetition, not motivation.

This is not for people looking for astrological prediction, personality validation, or inspiration without follow-through. It requires a willingness to follow a daily structure — even on the days you don't feel like it.
