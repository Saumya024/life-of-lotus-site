A structured behavioral reset built from psychological astrology.

It doesn't diagnose you or predict events. It helps you identify one repeating friction pattern and design a small, disciplined interruption around it — sustained for 43 days.

No grand promises. Just a clear structure and a commitment to follow it.

Short bursts create motivation. Sustained repetition creates change.

Forty-three days is long enough to outlast enthusiasm, resistance, and distraction — but short enough to commit to without much hassle. Most people quit between Day 12 and Day 21. This structure pushes beyond that drop-off point.

It's behavioral, not symbolic.

Identify your Ascendant/Moonsign in a Vedic chart. You define one daily action (15 minutes or less). You set a fixed time and minimum standard. You follow it for 43 days. You recalibrate on Day 7.

The action is small on purpose. Consistency builds structural force. Momentum comes from repetition, not motivation.

This is for people who want structure over motivation and are ready to follow through daily. It's ideal if you want a practical reset, clear discipline, and one small action you can sustain even on low-energy days.
